Practise Areas

Requirements capture, power budgeting, silicon selection and robust mixed-signal schematics built for review and reuse.

High-density digital, RF and precision analog layouts. Stackups, constraints and documentation tuned for your CM and test strategy.

Bring-up, instrumentation and test rigs that close the loop between theory, simulation and the very real, very noisy world.
